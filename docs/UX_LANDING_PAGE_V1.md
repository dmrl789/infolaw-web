# InfoLAW.net – Landing Page V1 (Spec)

## 1. Hero Section
- **Headline**: “Legal reasoning for AI, authored by jurists, rewarded fairly.”
- **Subheadline**: “Publish structured legal knowledge and datasets. Let AI agents and law firms reward your contributions with utility tokens fairly and with verifiable provenance.”
- **Primary CTAs**:
  - Button 1: “I’m a Jurist / Scholar” → scroll to jurist section or open application modal.
  - Button 2: “I Build AI Legal Tools” → scroll to AI section or open partner form.
- Background visual: stylized argument graph overlay + utility token motif.

## 2. How It Works
- **For Jurists (3 steps)**:
  1. Create a verified profile (bar ID, academic affiliation).
  2. Publish structured content using the FIRAC schema plus metadata.
  3. Earn fairly distributed utility tokens for each unlock, subscription, or bounty completion.
- **For AI Teams (3 steps)**:
  1. Browse curated collections filtered by jurisdiction/topic.
  2. License articles, datasets, or subscriptions through transparent utility token pricing.
  3. Integrate structured outputs via API/webhooks (coming soon).
- Layout: two parallel timelines with icons for each step.

## 3. For Jurists & Scholars
- Section title: “Turn your legal reasoning into on-chain income.”
- Bullet benefits:
  - Monetize deep legal analysis, case breakdowns, and benchmarks.
  - Maintain authorship & timestamp via HashTimer anchoring.
  - Contribute to safer, more transparent legal AI ecosystems.
- Include testimonial placeholder and screenshot slot of submission dashboard.

## 4. For AI Legal Agents & Firms
- Section title: “Fuel your AI counsel with curated legal corpora.”
- Bullet benefits:
  - Access structured datasets with clear provenance and licensing.
  - Quality signals from jurist verification, peer review, and reputation.
  - Ready-to-use benchmarks for evaluation and regression testing.
- Include logos placeholder for pilot partners.

## 5. Powered by fair utility tokens
- Short paragraph: “A fair utility token model powers InfoLAW transactions. Utility tokens settle every purchase, subscription, and bounty with negligible fees. Each dataset or article can be hashed and timestamped via HashTimer, ensuring immutable integrity checks for auditors and courts.”
- Visual: utility token icon + hash/timestamp motif.

## 6. Early Access / Waitlist
- Copy: “InfoLAW launches with an invite-only cohort of jurists and AI partners. Apply to secure your spot and influence the roadmap.”
- CTAs:
  - Button: “Apply as Jurist” → form collecting credentials, areas of law, preferred payment wallet.
  - Button: “Apply as AI Partner” → form collecting company, use case, dataset needs.
- Include FAQ teaser: “How do payouts work? What schemas are supported?” linking to docs.

## Layout Notes
- Single scrollable page with sticky top nav linking to each section.
- Color palette: deep navy + emerald (legal + blockchain cues), accent neon for utility token references.
- Typography: serif for headlines (heritage) and sans-serif for body (tech).
- Footer: email contact, provenance reference link, social placeholders.
